My name is Areté Poffart and this is my personal website. Here is a short bio, links to my social networks and further down a presentation of some projects that are under development.

Bio

I grew up in São Paulo, Brazil. I started in the world of programming at an early age. My first steps in programming logic were thanks to playing with Scratch and using ArduBlock to develop projects with Arduino. I chose to start with C programming between 12 and 13 years old. In the same period I started in the Linux world using Kali Linux. I am an enthusiast of the Linux ecosystem.

Self-taught, I learned about web development and application development in C, C++ and Python. At the age of 16 years, I have enhanced my knowledge in web and application development through my secondary-level Technical Diploma in Programming. I currently reside in Valdivia, Chile, where I am studying Electronic Engineering at Universidad Austral de Chile.

My primary interests lie at the intersection of mathematics and electronics, with a focus on scientific computing, low-level programming and embedded systems. In addition to my technical pursuits, I have a passion for digital illustration, landscape photography and astrophotography.
